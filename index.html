About

Hey, welcome to my site! My name is Max Krauskopf; thanks for stopping by to learn more about me. I am originally from just outside of Chicago, but currently reside in New York City. I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Emory University with a B.A. in economics and a minor in religion.

I am pursuing a career in fintech and product management. I have always been fascinated by financial markets, behavioral economics, and entrepreneurship. Currently, I am working at Stripe leading geographic expansion of their Issuing product, including stablecoin initiatives and international market development. I previously worked at HSBC where I helped manage the bank's relationships with Fortune 500 Consumer & Retail clients.

In my free time, I am an avid Formula One and hockey fan and enjoy activities such as skydiving, playing the guitar, traveling, and learning about new cultures.
